UN General Assembly 1st Committee

Combating the threat of bioterrorism by Non State Actors


In a world of rapid technological advancements, with global condemnation and the threat of mutually assured destruction from Nuclear Fallout, like a phoenix rising from its ashes, these ubiquitous threats make a comeback- pathogens and bioweapons. It’s what’s on the mind of every theatrical movie villain, and every radical resurgent militia, looking to take over the world and propagate their toxic beliefs. The first committee of the UNGA is hereby tasked with combating this rising threat- that may not be at the centre-stage but slips past our minds and into our lungs and is set to slowly cover the world with its hazardous fumes- Bioterrorism by non-state actors. Whether its US Postal System Anthrax attacks, Salmonella based food poisining by the Rajneeshee cult, weaponised Ebola or the pneumonic plague, the DISEC will have you delve into the details, don a biohazard suit, mask and gloves and fight threats the real way- creative solutions, counter-terrorism and government sanctioned surgical operations. Get ready for an action-packed General Assembly like truly never before.
