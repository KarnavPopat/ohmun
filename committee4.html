the united nations security council

International Peace and Security and the Right to self determination in the middle east.

The United Nations Security Council is one of the most vital organizations in the United Nations and is primarily tasked with the maintenance of peace and security across the globe. The SC has the power to take the lead with respect to constructive action, ranging from settling disputes using arbitration, or in extreme cases, sending in peace keeping troops. In the UNSC at OHMUN 2020, we will be discussing about the need for peace and security in the middle-eastern nations as well as the right to self-determination, owing to the conflict inflicted by militant groups over the years. Restraint and genuine dialogue are urgently needed, in order to avoid the risk that a minor miscalculation would inadvertently lead to a major confrontation, with disastrous consequences even well beyond the region.
